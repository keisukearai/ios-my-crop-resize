MyCropResize is a screenshot preparation tool built for iOS developers.

Crop and resize your screenshots to the exact dimensions required by the App Store — in just a few taps.

[Features]

■ Preset Resize
One-tap resize to standard App Store sizes: iPhone 6.7", 6.5", 5.5", iPad 13", and 12.9".

■ Free Crop
Drag to select any area and crop freely, preserving the natural aspect ratio of your selection.

■ JPEG / PNG Export
Choose your preferred format and save directly to your photo library.

■ Recent History
Quickly reopen recently edited images to speed up batch processing.

[Who it's for]
• Independent app developers
• Anyone who needs clean, correctly sized screenshots fast
• Those who prefer simple tools without unnecessary features

No ads. No in-app purchases. Completely free.
